Frequently Asked Questions
What is the P.A.R.T.S. Framework? ▾
P.A.R.T.S. stands for Persona, Aim, Recipients, Theme, and Structure. It's IteraAI's guided 5-step prompt engineering framework that helps CTE educators build powerful, standards-aligned AI prompts. Each step builds on the last to produce a complete, contextual prompt ready for Claude AI.
How does IteraAI support English Learners? ▾
IteraAI embeds EL support at every step. In Recipients, you select home languages and ELD levels to activate bilingual glossaries, parallel translations, and differentiated tiers. The Theme step includes SIOP/SDAIE strategies, and Structure offers 3-tiered differentiation and language objectives alongside content objectives.
What AI model powers IteraAI? ▾
IteraAI uses Anthropic's Claude AI to generate documents from your prompts. Claude is known for nuanced language capabilities and instructional depth, making it especially well-suited for education contexts.
Is my data saved? ▾
Prompts saved to My Library are stored in your browser's local storage. No account required during the pilot. Future versions will support cloud-based accounts for cross-device access. Connect form responses go directly to Juliano Calvo via email.
How do I get full access? ▾
IteraAI is in its pilot phase. Request access via the Connect page — Juliano reviews each request personally. If you received an access code, enter it in the Connect page's extension PIN field to unlock extended sessions.
